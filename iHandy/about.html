About Happy Hut

From pest contorl to plumbing services,electrcian we have high quality professionals. Happy Hut instantly matches thousands of customers every week with trusted professionals in cities all around the India.

Happy Hut Story

Happy Hut founded in 2014 is innovative approach to deal with problems for finding trusted professional, skilled workers for common household services. Sagar Gandewar came from United States in 2012 after staying for 5 plus years and found getting common household services done for his new apartment was cubersome and time consuming task. Even after spending considerable time in looking for skilled workers it is unclear if work will be done as promised. Bargains and negotiation remain even after task is done along with "Chai-Pani ke liye kuch toh de do" to end the day. (Tipping).

There was no easy way.

To fill this void Sagar & Pratik came with idea and Happy Hut was born, with the goal of building the easiest, most convenient way for busy people everywhere to book household services.
